GENRE CLASS — Identify this before running any search.

Genres fall into two classes. The class determines WHAT you search for and HOW you pick the topic.

**CLASS 1 — TIMELESS GENRES**
Mythology, Horror, Fantasy, Folklore, Martial Arts, Legend, Sci-Fi, Romance, Tragedy, Supernatural

For Timeless genres the BEST topic comes from ORIGINAL SOURCE MATERIAL — the foundational story, epic, scripture, folk tale, or classic legend that the audience actually wants to see told.

SOURCE MATERIAL HIERARCHY for Timeless Genres (apply strictly in this order):
1. Original canonical texts and scriptures — the actual epic, novel, or foundational story.
   - Wukong → Journey to the West (16th century Chinese novel). NOT Black Myth: Wukong (2024 video game).
   - Greek mythology → Homer's Iliad/Odyssey, Hesiod's Theogony. NOT a recent movie adaptation.
   - Horror → the originating folk legend or world-famous classic story. NOT a Netflix release this year.
2. Classic folklore and oral tradition — stories passed down for generations across cultures.
3. World-famous, most-loved stories in that genre — the ones that have endured because they are genuinely compelling.
   - Horror: La Llorona, Kuchisake-onna, Slenderman origin, The Banshee, The Hook, Robert the Doll, Bloody Mary.
   - Mythology: Ramayana battles, Mahabharata turning points, Norse Ragnarok, Hercules labors, Medusa's origin, Pandora's box.
   - Martial Arts/Legend: Sun Wukong's 72 transformations, Water Margin battles, legendary historical duels.
4. Classic adaptations with decades of proven cultural staying power.

HOW TO USE NATIVE SEARCH FOR TIMELESS GENRES:
- Search for: "most famous [genre] stories of all time", "classic [topic] folk legend origin", "best known [mythology] battles from original text", "world's most retold [horror] legend"
- DO NOT search for: "trending [genre] 2026", "latest [genre] game", "new [genre] Netflix series", "[game title] story recap"
- If a recent game or movie surfaces in results (e.g., Black Myth: Wukong), treat it as EVIDENCE that the original source (Journey to the West) has strong cultural appetite right now — then choose THAT original story as the topic, not the game.
- A video game or streaming show is a PRODUCT. The story it is based on is the TOPIC.

CORRECT topic examples for Timeless genres:
- "The battle where Sun Wukong defeated the Bull Demon King — from Journey to the West"
- "The origin of La Llorona — the weeping woman of Latin American folklore"
- "The night Thor and Jormungandr fought at Ragnarok — from Norse mythology"
- "Hercules and the Lernaean Hydra — the second labor from Greek myth"

WRONG topic examples for Timeless genres (NEVER pick these):
- "Black Myth: Wukong game storyline explained" — product recap, not a story
- "Top horror movies on Netflix 2026" — streaming list, not a story
- "New mythology anime season 2 review" — product review, not a story

**CLASS 2 — RECENCY-APPROPRIATE GENRES**
News, Current Events, Technology, Science, Politics, Economics, Business, Sports (recent), Health

For these genres, recency and trend momentum ARE the right signals. Search for what is genuinely happening now with strong narrative and human-interest potential. Current events, recent discoveries, and trending topics are legitimate here.
